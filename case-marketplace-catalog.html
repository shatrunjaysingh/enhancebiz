Cleaning a 4-million-listing marketplace catalog

Case Study · Retail & eCommerce · Representative engagement

This is a representative engagement that reflects the kind of work and results we deliver. Details are anonymized; swap in your real client story when ready.

The challenge

A fast-growing marketplace had millions of seller-created listings with inconsistent titles, missing attributes, miscategorized items, and a long tail of policy-violating content. Poor structure hurt search relevance; unmoderated content hurt buyer trust.

What we did

  • Attribute extraction — normalized brand, size, color, material, and category across the catalog to power faceted search.
  • Image & text moderation — reviewed listings against policy, flagging prohibited and misleading content.
  • Taxonomy mapping — recategorized mislabeled items into a consistent product taxonomy.
  • A feedback loop — high-frequency error patterns fed back into automated pre-checks so the human queue kept shrinking.
