How we solve the hard problems

Annotation quality lives and dies on process. Here's how AnnotateMe's features address the challenges that derail most projects.

Sensitive data

Tenant-isolated storage, role-based access, and full audit trails protect proprietary and personal data.

Annotator bias

Per-label guidelines shown in-editor plus 3-stage review minimize subjective drift.

Consistency at scale
