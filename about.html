About EnhanceBiz

We started out running operations for other people's businesses. Turns out that obsession with process is exactly what good AI data demands.

Our Story

From business process expertise to AI data leadership

EnhanceBiz began as a business process outsourcing company, delivering call center and customer support operations for global clients. As AI reshaped every industry, we saw where our strengths in disciplined, human-in-the-loop operations could matter most: building the training data behind modern machine learning.

Today we combine that operational heritage with our own technology — AnnotateMe, an AI-assisted annotation platform — to deliver annotation, NLP, and AI/ML dataset services at scale.
